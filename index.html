Welcome to AS Drilling Sloutions Inc

Founded in 2002, AS Drilling Sloutions Inc provides geological wellsite supervision, remote geosteering, reservoir evaluation, operations geology, oil sands coring supervision, petrographic analysis, geochemistry, engineering services and more for oil and gas operators in Canada, US and International markets. Over the last decade, we have grown from a start-up to a preferred contractor on many high profile projects. These projects include wildcat wells in the Aquitaine Basin in France, offshore exploration in Suriname, development wells in Albania, re-completions in Algeria and large delineation and development projects in the Athabasca oil sands and oil carbonates. We also have extensive experience across Western Canada, with an emphasis on unconventional resource plays such as Horn River, Montney, Duvernay and Cardium.
